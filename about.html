Brandofme.ai is an artificial-intelligence personal-branding platform that turns a name — or a single sentence — into a complete brand. From one prompt it can generate a brand name, a matching domain, a colour palette and type system, a website, short-form videos, social templates, a link-in-bio page and a brand voice, then package them together as a downloadable kit.

The platform was founded in June 2026 by Ghokkul Muhunthan, a software developer based in Auckland, New Zealand. Its stated mission is to let anyone build a credible, ownable brand in minutes — and to prove that world-class software can be built from anywhere in New Zealand.

Overview

Brandofme.ai positions itself as an end-to-end branding studio powered by AI. Where traditional branding requires a designer, a copywriter, a developer and weeks of back-and-forth, Brandofme aims to compress that into a single conversation: the user describes who they are or what they are building, and the platform returns a coherent identity across naming, design, content and web presence.

The product line spans a conversational brand generator, a prompt assistant, a video tool, a professional tier, a creator workspace, a template generator, a link-in-bio builder and a customisable bundle/plan. Each tool is designed to share the same underlying generative engine, so a brand created in one place stays consistent everywhere else.

History

Brandofme.ai was founded in June 2026 by Ghokkul Muhunthan. The idea grew out of a simple frustration: building a personal brand — a name, a logo, a website, a voice — was slow, expensive and intimidating for most people, even as AI was making every individual piece of it possible.

Ghokkul Muhunthan began studying software development at Auckland University of Technology in 2024, with a minor in astronomy and space science, before moving into postgraduate AI. Brandofme started as a series of small experiments in turning a single name into design — a colour, a monogram, a page — and gradually grew into a full platform that could generate an entire brand from a sentence.

How it works

Across its products, Brandofme follows the same three-step pattern:

  • Describe. The user types a name, a sentence or a few words about who they are or what they are building.
  • Generate. The engine returns a complete, coherent brand — name, domain ideas, palette, type, website, templates, voice and social handles.
  • Claim & use. The user can refine the result, claim their unique domain and full brand kit for $10, and carry the identity into every other product.

The Engine

All Brandofme products are built on a shared generative engine. It reads a prompt, infers a subject and a tone, and produces a brandable name, a harmonious colour palette, a font pairing, a monogram and supporting copy. Because every product draws on the same engine, a brand created in Conjure or Brand Studio carries cleanly into Video Studio, Template.ai and the Link-in-bio page without losing consistency.

Pricing

Brandofme is designed to be approachable. Generating a brand is free to try, and the core offer is a one-time $10 AI Brand Bundle — a unique domain plus the complete brand kit. Beyond that, the Bundle plan-builder lets users scale individual products (more videos, more brand projects, more workspace automations) and see their monthly total update live.
